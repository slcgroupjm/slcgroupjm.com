Hurricane Melissa Donation Drive

Our Mission

At SLC Foundation, our mission is to serve communities through compassion, partnership, and meaningful action. We are committed to supporting individuals and families during times of hardship by bringing people together to provide hope, encouragement, and essential assistance. Through community-driven initiatives, we strive to make a lasting difference and remind those in need that they are supported.

The Impact

Following the impact of Hurricane Melissa, SLC was honoured to partner with The House of God Church, Bog Walk; Y&C Tours; and Upliftment Early Childhood Learning Centre from Portmore in a humanitarian effort to support families in Cane Cunnan, Westmoreland. Through the generosity and dedication of our partners, donors, and volunteers, we were able to provide assistance to over 50 children and families affected by the hurricane. In keeping with our commitment to dignity and respect, we chose not to record photographs or videos of the families and children we served in order to protect their privacy and preserve their personal experiences.
This initiative demonstrated the power of community coming together in times of need. Beyond the support provided, it represented a message of hope, care, and solidarity for those affected. SLC Foundation continues to believe in serving with compassion, protecting the dignity of those we assist, and creating meaningful impact through partnership and service.
