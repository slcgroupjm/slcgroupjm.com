KEY ADMISSIONS INFORMATION

Sunshine Learning Center (SLC) is proud of the diverse and inclusive learning environment we provide. We are committed to ensuring that every child is welcomed, supported, and encouraged to grow, learn, and develop to their fullest potential, regardless of their background or ability.

SLC operates under the SLC Group (of schools) and follows an admissions policy that promotes fairness, and equal opportunity for all families. Our aim is to ensure that every child has access to a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where early learning can flourish.

To support families, we also offer flexible payment plans, making quality early childhood education more accessible while ensuring that every child has the opportunity to benefit from our programs.

All prospective parents wishing to apply to any branch of SLC Group (Early Childhood or Preparatory) can arrange their visit to any school via email or phone to complete the registration form. You can find a list of required documents for each institution on our website, or by clicking one of the buttons below.

ADDITIONAL POLICIES

ABNORMAL APPLICATIONS

Waiting Lists & Off-Season Registration

If you wish to apply to one of our institutions after registration has closed or before it has opened for the next Academic Year, please reach out to the respective branch (Early Childhood or Preparatory) and schedule a meeting with the Director/Principal.

If all available places are filled, families may request to have their child’s name placed on the waiting list. The waiting list is maintained on a first come, first served basis, based on the date the application is received.

When a place becomes available, it will be offered to the next child on the waiting list. If the place is accepted, the remaining children on the list will move up accordingly.

In special circumstances, the school reserves the right to prioritise a child where it is considered necessary, for example in cases relating to welfare or other exceptional considerations.

Specific Learning & Accessibility Accommodations

If your child has specific learning disabilities or other disabilities that need to be accommodated, please give us a call or send an email to find out more about our accessibilities and how we can help.

UPCOMING EVENT

ORIENTATION DAY

To truly experience what it means to be part of the SLC Group, we invite all registered parents to join us for our upcoming orientation days.

We believe that a strong partnership between home and school is the cornerstone of your child’s success, and these sessions are designed to build that foundation from the very beginning.

During your visit, you will have the chance to connect with our staff, explore our core programmes, and tour our learning environments.
