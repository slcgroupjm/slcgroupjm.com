WHO WE ARE

Building More Than Schools. Building Futures.

Sunshine Learning Center Group is an independent educational organization committed to developing children and young people from their earliest years through secondary education while extending our impact into communities through meaningful outreach initiatives.

Established in 2020, the Group was founded on the belief that education should nurture academic excellence, strong character, leadership, creativity, and lifelong learning. What began as one early childhood institution has grown into a family of educational divisions serving learners at every stage of development.

Our Purpose

To provide a complete educational pathway where students are supported, challenged, and inspired to become confident learners, responsible citizens, and leaders of character.

OUR STORY

From One School
To One Educational Family

Sunshine Learning Center was birthed out of the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic when many parents experienced challenges locating a safe, reliable, and nurturing learning environment for their children.

Recognizing this need, the Founder established Sunshine Learning Center on September 7, 2020, with the vision of creating an institution where children could develop academically, socially, emotionally, and creatively.

As the institution continued to grow, the vision expanded beyond early childhood education into creating a complete educational pathway that supports students from their earliest years through secondary education and beyond.

OUR VALUES

What We Stand For

Every division of Sunshine Learning Center Group is united by shared values that guide our approach to education, leadership, and service.

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in education, leadership, and service while continuously improving our practices.

Integrity

We encourage honesty, responsibility, and ethical decision-making in all areas of our organization.

Respect

We value every student, family, educator, and community member by creating an environment of dignity and inclusion.

Leadership

We develop confident individuals who are prepared to positively influence their communities and the world.

Innovation

We embrace creativity, technology, and new approaches that improve learning experiences.

Community

We believe education extends beyond the classroom through service, partnerships, and meaningful impact.
