Annual Strathmore Children's Home Christmas Charity

Our Mission

At SLC Foundation, our mission is to bring hope, joy, and meaningful support to children and families within our communities. Through the generosity of our parents, partners, and donors, we strive to create opportunities that allow children to feel loved, valued, and celebrated during important moments throughout the year. Our annual Christmas initiative at Strathmore Children's Home reflects our commitment to spreading kindness and making a lasting impact in the lives of children.

The Impact

For the 5th consecutive year, SLC Foundation, with the generous support of parents at SLC and other valued donors, was able to bring the joy and spirit of Christmas to the children of Strathmore Children's Home. Through the contributions received, we provided the children with a memorable Christmas celebration filled with care, generosity, and moments of happiness. This initiative goes beyond gifts and celebrations—it represents the power of community coming together to show children that they are remembered, appreciated, and supported. Each year, the continued generosity of our SLC family and donors allows us to strengthen our commitment to serving others and creating positive experiences that leave a lasting impression.
