SUNSHINE LEARNING CENTER

Sunshine Learning Center was birthed out of the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic. The Principal and founder saw the need to provide a learning facility and internet centre to aid many parents who had difficulty in locating a facility that was able to provide care and nurture to their children's young minds.

For a very long time, parents of this country have longed for a dedicated institution which understands their child's needs and developmental stages. As such, Sunshine Leaning Center began on September 7, 2020, under the motto "Building Great Minds for a Brighter Future"

Our Mission
Statement

Our mission is to create a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children from 6 months to 6 years are encouraged to explore, discover, and learn. Through meaningful experiences and developmentally appropriate activities, we promote readiness in literacy and numeracy while supporting each child's physical, mental, and emotional growth. In doing so, we strive to establish ourselves as a leader in early childhood education in Jamaica.

Our Vision
Statement

Our vision is to deliver high-quality education at a reasonable cost while building a financially stable and self-sufficient institution. We are committed to empowering and developing our staff and students as we strive to become the school of choice within our cluster, parish, and across the country. Through strong leadership and effective management, we aim to exceed all operational standards, including those set by the ECC, while consistently achieving meaningful results and maintaining excellence in everything we do.
