SUNSHINE LEARNING CENTER KINDERGARTEN & PREPARATORY

The story of Sunshine Learning Center (SLC) is one of quiet perseverance, unwavering faith in Christ, and a deep-seated commitment to the children of Jamaica. We didn't just want to start a school; we wanted to become the pioneers of a new standard in education, helping parents find a place where their children aren't just taught, but truly seen and empowered.

For over half a decade, our Early Childhood division has served as a beam of light for young learners. Today, we stand on a foundation of proven results. Our ECI graduates are known for being independent readers by age five and confident, inquisitive leaders. It was the overwhelming support and testimonials from our parents who witnessed this transformation and insisted on a continuation of our work—that birthed the next stage of our evolution: Sunshine Learning Center Kindergarten & Preparatory.

Our Mission
Statement

Our mission is to provide a supportive and engaging learning environment where students are encouraged to grow academically, socially, and personally. Through high-quality instruction, meaningful learning experiences, and strong values, we aim to strengthen literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking skills while preparing students for the next stage of their educational journey. By nurturing confidence, curiosity, and character, we strive to help every child discover their potential and build pathways to endless possibilities.

Our Vision
Statement

Our vision is to become a leading preparatory institution known for academic excellence, strong character development, and a commitment to student success. We aspire to create an environment where students are empowered to think independently, pursue knowledge with confidence, and develop the skills needed to thrive in an ever-changing world. ]Through innovation, dedicated staff, and strong partnerships with families, we aim to prepare students not only for their next educational step, but for lifelong achievement.
