Understanding Our Fees

We believe in making our fee structure clear and transparent for every family. Below is a breakdown of the required fees, payment methods, and answers to our most commonly asked questions.

School Fee / Tuition

School or tuition fees are paid termly or yearly at the beginning of each payment period.

Available payment methods include:

  • Online bank transfer (online website payments coming soon)
  • Cash payments at the school
  • Debit/Credit Card using our Point of Sale (POS)
  • Bank Deposit

Lunch Fee

Lunch fees are paid in full in cash at the beginning of each four-week period.

M.I.P Protection Package

M.I.P stands for Maintenance, Insurance & PTA. This is a one-time annual fee paid at the beginning of each school year.

The package covers:

  • School maintenance
  • PTA contributions
  • Student accident insurance required by the Ministry of Education

Students cannot be enrolled without this insurance coverage.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if my child wasn't enrolled in September? +
Regardless of when your child is enrolled during the school year, the M.I.P fee must still be paid. For example, if your child enrolled in January, the fee is payable upon registration and again in September should they continue attending school.
Can I only pay the Insurance portion of M.I.P? +
No. The M.I.P Protection Package must be paid in full and cannot be separated into individual components.
If my child is absent, will tuition be refunded? +
In exceptional circumstances such as a critical illness resulting in ten (10) or more consecutive excused absences, tuition for the missed days will be credited to the next billing period. A medical report or physician's sick leave certificate is required. Credits apply only when tuition for the current period has been paid in full.
Why do payment options have different fees? +
  • Cash payments at school incur no additional fee.
  • Bank deposits are subject to your bank's charges.
  • Online bank transfers may attract ACH or RTGS fees depending on your financial institution.
  • Website online checkout may include applicable GCT.
  • Payments made through the school's POS terminal include a service fee.
